Problems + proposed plans
🔴 Blocked — Engineering cannot start
Periodization methodology is not yet documented
The adaptive engine — the core differentiator of this product — runs on the client's periodization rules. Zone prescriptions, readiness adjustment logic, and RPE-driven overload calculations must be extracted and documented before any engineering work can begin. Currently, this exists only in the client's head.
Proposed Plan
Schedule a 90-minute methodology extraction session immediately after the discovery call. Use a structured interview format to document: (1) zone prescription rules, (2) readiness scoring logic, (3) RPE-to-overload formulas. Output becomes the engineering spec for the adaptive engine. Without this session, V1 engineering cannot start.
Needs Decision
🟡 At Risk — Scope clarity needed
Four major features competing for V1 — all four is 12–18 months
The current vision includes: (1) adaptive coaching platform, (2) motion capture / biomechanics layer, (3) travel kit consumer funnel, and (4) team/group coaching. Each is a meaningful build on its own. Attempting all four in V1 creates a timeline the business cannot support.
Proposed Plan
Recommend V1 = adaptive coaching platform only (check-ins, RPE, coach dashboard, client app, billing). Motion capture → V2. Travel kit funnel → V2 or parallel lightweight build. Team coaching → V2. This gets a working, differentiated product to real clients in 90–120 days instead of 18 months.
Needs Decision
🟡 At Risk — Expectation gap
Motion capture on a phone camera may not meet accuracy expectations
Phone-based pose estimation is technically feasible but clinical-grade biomechanical accuracy is not achievable on standard hardware. If the client's expectation is VALD-level precision from a phone, this needs to be addressed before it becomes a scope dispute mid-build.
Proposed Plan
On the discovery call, clarify the accuracy bar explicitly. Present two options: (A) phone-based, catches obvious form breakdowns, not clinical-grade — ships in V2 at lower cost, or (B) hardware-assisted, clinical accuracy — significantly higher cost and complexity. Get a clear choice on record.
Needs Input
🔵 FYI — No action needed yet
Travel kit funnel timing is unknown — may force V1 scope change
If the travel kit is already in market and customers are downloading the app expecting a self-guided experience, the consumer funnel moves from "future phase" to "V1 requirement." We don't yet know the kit's market status.
Proposed Plan
Discovery call will answer this. If kit is already in market, self-guided mode gets added to V1 scope and timeline adjusts accordingly. If kit is not yet launched, consumer funnel stays in V2 and we proceed with coaching platform only.
